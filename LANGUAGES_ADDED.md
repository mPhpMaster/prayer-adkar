# New Languages Added to ADHKAR App

## Languages Added for Countries with Large Muslim Populations

The app now supports **10 languages** (previously 3):

### Original Languages:
1. **العربية (Arabic)** - Middle East, North Africa
2. **English** - International
3. **ไทย (Thai)** - Thailand

### New Languages Added:
4. **Indonesia (Indonesian)** - Indonesia has the world's largest Muslim population (~230 million Muslims)
5. **اردو (Urdu)** - Pakistan (~220 million Muslims), India
6. **বাংলা (Bengali)** - Bangladesh (~150 million Muslims), India
7. **Türkçe (Turkish)** - Turkey (~85 million Muslims)
8. **فارسی (Persian/Farsi)** - Iran (~85 million Muslims), Afghanistan
9. **Melayu (Malay)** - Malaysia (~20 million Muslims), Brunei, Singapore
10. **Français (French)** - Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia, Senegal, Mali, and other French-speaking African countries with large Muslim populations

## Language Order
Languages are displayed in the following order:
1. العربية (Arabic) - Islamic primary language
2. English - International
3. ไทย (Thai)
4. Indonesia
5. اردو (Urdu)
6. বাংলা (Bengali)
7. Türkçe (Turkish)
8. فارسی (Farsi)
9. Melayu (Malay)
10. Français (French)

## Full Translations
Each language includes complete translations for:
- App title and subtitle
- All 6 dhikr types (Subhanallah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu Akbar, La ilaha illallah, Astaghfirullah, La hawla)
- Counter interface
- All buttons (Count, Reset, View Statistics, Clear All Data)
- Statistics page
- Alert dialogs
- Language selector

## RTL (Right-to-Left) Support
Languages with RTL support:
- Arabic (ar)
- Urdu (ur)
- Persian/Farsi (fa)

## Coverage
These 10 languages cover Muslims in countries representing approximately **1.2 billion** of the world's 1.8 billion Muslims (~67% coverage).
